## About

**AI Funding** ([aifunding.me](https://aifunding.me)) is the most comprehensive AI venture funding database. We track every AI startup funding round — from pre-seed to mega-rounds — with verified data, source links, and investor details.

### Key Numbers

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Companies tracked | 132+ |
| Funding rounds | 148+ |
| Active investors | 48+ |
| Total funding tracked | $55.8B+ |
| Data freshness | Updated daily |

## Features

### For Investors & Analysts
- **[Live Deals Feed](https://aifunding.me/deals)** — every AI funding round, grouped by week, filterable by sector and stage
- **[Company Profiles](https://aifunding.me/companies)** — full funding history with source links, investor details, cumulative totals, and valuations
- **[Top AI Startups](https://aifunding.me/top-ai-startups)** — leaderboard ranked by total capital raised
- **[Sector Analysis](https://aifunding.me/sector/ai-infrastructure)** — 16 canonical AI sectors with company counts and funding totals
- **[Data Explorer](https://aifunding.me/data-explorer)** — filter, sort, and analyze the complete dataset
- **[AI Funding Insights](https://aifunding.me/insights)** — in-depth analysis, reports, and funding trend research

### Data Quality
- Every funding round includes a verifiable source link to the original article
- Company profiles show the full funding journey with cumulative totals
- Valuations sourced from public disclosures or estimated using industry-standard methodology
- Automated quality checks run daily to ensure data accuracy
